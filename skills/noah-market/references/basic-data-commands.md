# Basic Data

## get-capital-flow

- 命令：`noah market get-capital-flow`
- 查看详情：`noah inspect market get-capital-flow`
- 摘要：获取个股资金流向
- 标签：Basic Data
- 鉴权：需要 bearer 鉴权。

---

## get-market-state

- 命令：`noah market get-market-state`
- 查看详情：`noah inspect market get-market-state`
- 摘要：获取股票对应市场的市场状态
- 标签：Basic Data
- 鉴权：需要 bearer 鉴权。
